From the expansive estates of Newport come the women with expensive taste- The Old Money Honeys are prim and hardly proper in their white tennis skirts and pearls. Daddy's money may not buy happiness for these firece ladies on the rink, but mommy's attitude cetainly packs a mean (and painful!) hit. They'll run you off the rink as fast as they'll run you out of their prestigious country clubs!
