PRIVACY POLICY
Providence Roller Derby, LLC will NEVER use your personal information in a way that violates your privacy.

1. We will not share or sell your personal information with a third party in the normal course of doing business. We may share personal information with law enforcement, government officials, or other third parties when:
- we are compelled to do so by a subpoena, court order or similar legal procedure
- we need to do so to comply with law
- we believe in good faith that the disclosure of personal information is necessary to prevent physical harm or financial loss, to report suspected illegal activity, or to investigate violations of our User Agreement.

2. All monetary transactions through our site will be powered through PayPal to ensure absolutely secure purchases of merchandise. View the PayPal Privacy Policy

3. We will not use your information in a way that harrasses you or causes you distress, and you may request to be removed from mailing lists or contact databases at any time by emailing any of the contacts listed here.
