PRD'S STRIPE BRIGADE
No one would be able to follow roller derby if it weren't for our referees. They keep points, track penalties, and eject those extra-saucy ladies putting too much smack down. Some of our refs are skaters from men's and co-ed roller derby leagues, coaches at league practices, and sometimes a retired or guest skater will pop by to throw on some stripes and lay down the law of the rink.
