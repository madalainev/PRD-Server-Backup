The Rhode Island Riveters are Providence Roller Derby's All-Star Travel Team. Composed of the best in the league, they compete nationally in individual bouts as well as multi-bout tournaments. The Riveters consistently rank in the top 20 leagues in the Nation (out of the approximately 55 WFTDA leagues). Inspired by Rosie the Riveter's work ethic, these ladies practice hard, skate hard, and plan on bringing home win after win for the Ocean State! We Can Do It!
