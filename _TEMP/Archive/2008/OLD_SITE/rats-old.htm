Out of the sewers of Rhode Island crawl the Sakonnet River Roller Rats in their mutant green body socks... teeth bared and claws out! The Rats have a reputation as the nastiest, dirtiest, and most offensive team on Providence Roller Derby. Now, whether they bite, scratch, or hoard their way to a win on the track... the Rats aren't going to be worried about what anyone above the sewer grate says.
