Rules of the Game



Roller derby is one of the fastest growing female sports. Providence Roller Derby has an open enlistment policy for skaters and referees. No matter what your skill level, we will teach and train you to be the toughest skater you can be. If you are interested in becoming a skater, please contact Foxie Renard at FoxieRenard147@gmail.com to find out how to join. We will be hosting a series of open practices over the off-season for eveyone to come and check us out. We are also able to provide equipment to skaters until they are able to purchase their own.

Don't want to compete but still want in on the action? Become a referee. Referees are comprised of men and women and can participate in practices, scrimmages, and bouts. Contact Crash Daily at emailcrash@gmail.com for more information!
