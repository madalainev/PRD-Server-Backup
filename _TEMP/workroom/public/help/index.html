OpenGoo Help

OpenGoo is a complex application developed with the effort of many contributors from the open source community. A manual is constantly being developed by volunteers. When the OpenGoo manual is ready it will be available at OpenGoo's site at wiki.opengoo.org.

You can get help and and answers to your questions at the OpenGoo forums: forums.opengoo.org.

If you want to help OpenGoo become the best WebOffice contact us at contact@opengoo.org or through the forums. You can help by reporting bugs, giving us your suggestions and feedback or contributing with your knowledge in either programming, documentation writing, graphic design, marketing, etc.

Thank you for using OpenGoo!
